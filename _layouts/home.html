Ready to take the next step?

Visit iOS Lab on Tuesdays at 5:00pm in CCB 340. It’s a new initiative we’re starting for intermediate/advanced iOS developers to come in and work on their projects. This includes getting time to build your own apps, viewing presentations on advanced topics, participating in discussions, and getting development help.

Learn about the basics in iOS Club and get a feel for mobile development.

Step 2

Build your own apps, get more practice, and expand on your knowledge in iOS Lab.

Become an officer and teach the next generation or land an internship and gain industry experience.
